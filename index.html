Welcome to GEONESYS!!!

This is a 2 player game. It's played on the same PC, so ask a buddy to play with you.
When you are not forever alone, you can continue reading

Ok it's a battle. There are two facions: Inquisitors versus Revels.
Each faction controls an army formed by 3 heroes, one of them is the Leader.
Inquisitors play at the left side of the screen. Revels to the right.
Revels deploy their army first. Inquisitors have the first turn of the battle.

Choose your side and KILL THE ENEMY LEADER before yours gets killed!!!

REMEMBER:
There can only be 2 heroes in the same zone
Cats are dangerous, they can throw hairballs...
Dogs are tough (and lovely <3)
Batman and Superman, well, they are great
The Leaders improve when they're choosen
