Welcome to GEONESYS!!!
This is a 2 player game. Inquisitors versus Revels. Choose your side!!!
Inquisitors play at the left side of the screen. Revels to the right. Sit down properly.
Each player controls an army formed by 3 heroes, one of them is the Leader.
Try to kill the enemy Leader before yours gets killed!!!
